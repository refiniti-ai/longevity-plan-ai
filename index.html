Frequently Asked Questions

What is LongevityPlan.AI? +
LongevityPlan.AI is a precision health platform that combines advanced AI analytics, biomarker tracking, and expert coaching to create personalized longevity protocols designed to extend your healthspan and optimize performance.
Who is this program designed for? +
Our platform is designed for high-performing executives, athletes, and proactive individuals who want data-driven insights to optimize their biology, improve resilience, and extend their healthy years.
How does the "Digital Twin" work? +
We integrate data from wearables, lab tests, and lifestyle inputs to create a "digital twin" of your health. Our AI then generates dynamic, daily recommendations for nutrition, sleep, and exercise, supported by human coaching.
Do I need to be an athlete? +
No, you don't need to be an athlete. While we serve elite performers, our protocols are customized to your unique baseline and goals, whether that's managing executive stress, improving energy, or simply living healthier for longer.
How does the API integration integrate with existing EHRs? +
Our API uses standard FHIR-compliant data structures, making integration with major Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems like Epic and Cerner seamless and secure.
Is the platform HIPAA and SOC2 compliant? +
Yes, our entire infrastructure is HIPAA compliant and SOC2 Type II certified, ensuring the highest standards of data privacy and security for your organization.
Can we white-label the coaching interface? +
Absolutely. Our enterprise solution allows full white-labeling of both the user interface and the generated health reports, maintaining your brand identity throughout the user journey.
What kind of support is available for enterprise integration? +
We provide a dedicated technical account manager and 24/7 developer support to assist with API implementation, data mapping, and custom feature development.
